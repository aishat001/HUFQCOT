About HUFQCOT

This foundation was created for only Islamic Schoos in order to elevate the Qur'an memorization of our students through a standard curriculum and to end up all the problems or challenges they do face to memorize the glorious Qur'an. NB: Therefore, you are all free to ask any question or share problems your SCHOOL might be facing with us in order to have an adequate advise and for the total solutions.

  • BENEFITS
  • Will unite the Islamic Schools in the west.
  • Will fast-track the memorization of the students.
  • Will enlightened the school management in the field of Qu'ran.
  • School in need of a qualified hafeez can ask us for recruitment.
  • Musabaqoh (for schools in the west only, like it is been done in the north) will be organized to keep our students in focused and the school hafeez will be forced to teach their students up to date of the curriculum given to them. Even the student will also be forced to do their muraji'ah (revision) when they know they will be g musabaqoh. Therefore, the problem of muraji'ah will definitely be solved. Also this will reduce the management's effort in monitoring their hafeez for muraji'ah because they also knew they will be having a competition ahead.
  • The rewards that awaits us.
